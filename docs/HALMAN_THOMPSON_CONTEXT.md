## Origin Story

Founded during the first COVID lockdown (2020) when the team experimented with techniques to create artisan finishes on solid brass and copper sheets. The company evolved from the existing Amerex Group industrial metal supply chain — they already had raw material access, cutting infrastructure (guillotines), and logistics. Halman Thompson is the premium/luxury offshoot targeting design-conscious consumers and trade professionals.

## Sister Company

**Online Metal Ltd** (buymetalonline.co.uk) — B2C sheet metal supply business, also Newcastle-based. Founded 2013 by Sara Halman Thompson and brother Ross Halman. Supplies cut-to-size mild steel, stainless steel, aluminium, brass, copper, and corten. Over 10,000 orders by 2019. Feeds raw materials to Halman Thompson and cross-refers customers.

- **Turnaround:** 5-8 weeks standard

- **Process:** Laser cut to size → hand-aged → lacquered (matte or gloss) → packaged
- **Shipping:** Pieces > 1300mm ship on pallet (£50 surcharge)
